Design a magical fantasy birthday website inspired by a wizard school theme.

The website is a surprise birthday experience for a girl named Akhila.

Overall Mood:
Cinematic, magical, emotional, dreamy.
Dark navy and deep purple night theme.
Golden glowing magical accents.
Floating candles.
Soft sparkles and particle magic effects.
Elegant serif magical typography.

Full Story Flow:

1) Landing Scene – The Magical Letter

Dark night background with a castle silhouette in distance.
Floating candles in the air.
An owl flying across screen delivering a glowing parchment letter.
The parchment slowly opens with a magical glow.

Text on letter:
“Dear Akhila,
You are invited to a magical celebration tonight.”

Below that:
A glowing gold button: “Open The Magical Portal”

Add soft magical sparkles around the button.


2) Scene Two – The Great Hall Preparation

User enters a grand magical hall inspired by a wizard castle.
Floating candles above.
Long hall with warm golden lighting.
Magical sparkles floating in air.

Characters (silhouettes, wizard students) appear to be secretly arranging decorations.
Decor includes glowing lights and floating ribbons.
Atmosphere feels like a surprise is about to happen.

Subtle fantasy orchestral background mood.


3) Scene Three – Magical Spell to Create Cake

Lights dim slightly.
A wand appears in center of screen.
Magical glowing particles swirl around it.

Text appears:
“Let’s create something magical…”

Spell text appears in glowing gold letters:
“Celebrata Lumina!”

When wand is clicked:
Golden and blue particles swirl and form a beautiful magical birthday cake in the center.

The cake slowly materializes from sparkles.
Candles appear on top.
Another spell text appears:
“Ignis Lumos!”
Candles light automatically with small flame animation.


4) Scene Four – The Grand Reveal

Screen goes slightly dark.
Suddenly fireworks explode in magical colors (gold, pink, purple).

Large glowing golden text appears in center:

✨ Happy Birthday Akhila ✨

Crackers and sparkles burst in background.
Confetti slowly falls.
Magical glow around text.
Dreamy cinematic lighting.


5) Final Scene – Video Message

After fireworks settle,
A glowing magical frame appears.

Text above it:
“One more message from someone special…”

A video placeholder area inside glowing fantasy frame.
Soft golden glow border.
Floating sparkles around video.

Below video:
Text:
“May all your dreams shine brighter than the stars.”

Make the entire website feel like an interactive magical birthday story experience, not a normal webpage.
